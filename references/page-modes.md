# Page Modes and Weights

Choose the page type before computing the overall score.

## Blog

Use `blog` when the page's primary job is education, discovery, thought leadership, or organic acquisition through informational intent.

Weights:

- SEO: `30%`
- GEO: `25%`
- AEO: `25%`
- LLMO: `20%`

Bias notes:

- Reward internal links, topical completeness, FAQ quality, and evidence density.
- Penalize thin introductions, hidden answers, and generic commentary.

## Landing Page

Use `landing-page` when the page's primary job is conversion, lead capture, demo booking, or offer explanation.

Weights:

- SEO: `25%`
- GEO: `20%`
- AEO: `25%`
- LLMO: `30%`

Bias notes:

- Reward clear offer framing, objections handled in-text, proof, and retrieval-friendly sections.
- Penalize pages that rely on visuals or slogans without enough textual specificity.

## Other

Use `other` for homepages, service hubs, category pages, or mixed-intent assets that do not fit neatly into blog or landing-page behavior.

Weights:

- SEO: `30%`
- GEO: `25%`
- AEO: `20%`
- LLMO: `25%`

Bias notes:

- Keep the score conservative.
- Explain which page behaviors made classification ambiguous.
